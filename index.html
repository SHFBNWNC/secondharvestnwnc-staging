Mobile pantry

Our home

A space built with the same care as our mission.

Volunteer lounge
The Piedmont Room
Produce mural hallway

Our 140,000 sq. ft. facility in Winston-Salem was designed to feel like a place you want to be — not a place you have to be. Powered by the largest rooftop solar array in North Carolina through our SolarHarvest sustainability program, it's also home to a full-service community event center with in-house catering by our award-winning culinary team and complete AV service.
